NEWS

According to the Chinese economic news network, according to the world travel and Tourism Council (WTTC) of the 2017 annual global economic impact of tourism and the University of Oxford in the UK in March 22nd released the latest show: in 2017, the global tourism industry growth rate of 4.6%, the growth rate exceeded the manufacturing industry (4.2%), retail and wholesale trade (3.4%), agriculture, forestry and Fisheries (2.6%) and financial services (2.5%), the tourism industry has more than 7 consecutive years of global economic growth, has become the world's fastest growing economy industry.

China's tourism growth is ahead of the world

The research report released the 2017 fastest growing ten tourism countries in the world: China, the United States, Britain, Turkey, Egypt, India, Spain, Japan, Italy and Germany. Among them, China is leading the world with a growth rate of 9.8%.

The new consumer driven Chinese tourism industry into the new journey

In the past New Year's day, the number and income of domestic tourism have achieved two digit growth. According to the comprehensive measurement of the data center of the National Tourism Administration, the total number of domestic tourists was 133 million, which increased by 11.08% over the 2018 New Year holidays. The domestic tourism revenue reached 75 billion 500 million yuan, up 11.22% over the same period last year.
In the first half of 2017, the number of domestic tourists was up to 2 billion 537 million, and the tourism income was 2 trillion and 170 billion yuan. Throughout the year, although the specific data have not yet been published, the number of tourist arrivals in China's tourism industry in 2017 was more than 5 billion people without pressure. In the context of the continued prosperity of tourism consumption, China's tourism industry in the past year has been moving forward at a high speed. Transportation, accommodation, catering and scenic spots have shown a global tourism trend, and tens of millions of specific projects have updated the form of blending with other industries.

The top 5 fastest growing cities in the world are all in China

According to the CNBC report on October 25th, according to the latest research report of the World Tourism Council, the five fastest growing cities in the world are all in China. The Council's latest "urban tourism impact" report shows that Asian cities will continue to lead the tourism industry in the next ten years, especially in Chinese cities. Chongqing, the city of southwestern China, ranked the top of the list of the latest research, up 14%, followed by 13.1% in Guangzhou. The report said, "the strong development of Chongqing and Guangzhou has been supported by China's strong domestic market, and domestic tourists account for 94.5% and 89.1% of the total tourism industry of two cities". Shanghai's growth rate also reached 12.8%, and Beijing, the capital, was 12%. Gloria Guevara, the president of the World Tourism Council, said, "the growth of the two figures in this region is really amazing."

TREND

  • China's tourism industry started late, before the founding of new China, China's economic depression, tourism development is basically stagnant, tourism industry has not yet formed. Since the reform and opening up, with the sustained and rapid development of China's economy and the rapid increase of the residents' income level, the number of tourist and tourism income has been increasing rapidly. The tourism industry has become an important industry of the national economy, and has become one of the fastest growing residents' consumption areas. The National Tourism Administration "Chinese Tourism Statistical Bulletin" data show that from 2006 to 2015 of ten years, the domestic tourism revenue overall showing steady growth, income rose from 622 billion 970 million yuan in 2006 to 3 trillion and 419 billion 510 million yuan in 2015, the annual compound growth rate of 19%.

  • In recent years, the development of China's leisure and vacation tourism is fast, but tourism is still in the dominant position. Its activities are relatively simple, and there are few tourist attractions. The tourist area and time period, generally in the short distance travel, long-distance travel is relatively less; travel time is shorter, and more generally for two or three or three days, five days, one day tour also occupies a large proportion, more than a week to travel less. At present, China's tourism industry is still in the stage of the transition from sightseeing to leisure tourism, and it has not really entered the era of leisure and holiday tourism.

  • Look at the cost per capita from tourism, tourism spending per capita in nearly ten years to maintain rapid growth, the urban residents per capita travel cost from 985.50 yuan in 2006 increased to $766.40 in 2015 an increase of 29%; rural residents per capita travel costs increased from 221.90 yuan in 2006 to 2015 554.20 yuan, an increase of 149.75%, rural residents, tourism spending per capita growth rate far more than the growth rate of urban residents, urban residents and tourism spending per capita is narrowing the gap.
